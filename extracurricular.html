Extracurriculars

Outside of school I participate in many activites. I take voice lessons, swim competively, take dance, and do musical theatre.

Voice Lessons

In the summer of 2020 I started taking lessons with Talamantes Wilkerson Voice Studio. I have studied tradtional Italian songs and arias. This year I placed 3rd in my category for my NATS competition.

Competitive Swim

I've been swimming competitvely for 9 years now. I started in kindergarden swimming for the Potomac Marlins, in 2016 I swam for the Marlins in the annual Winter Classic. In 2018 I decided to switch to Herdon Aquatic Club where I'm still swimming now. In addition to doing year round swim I swim on my summer swim team, the Chantilly Highland Dolphins. In 2012 I was on the all star team and have been to divisonals every year since I was on the team.

Dance

Starting from 2019 I've taken dance classes at Not Just Dance. I taken jazz 1 and 2. Besides from taking dace there I've learned a lot of other things about dance from watching different YouTube videos.

Musical Theatre

I have been a memeber of Not Just Dance for 3 years now. I have preformed as Chi Fu in Mulan JR, Zeke in High School Musical JR, Bobby in A Chorus Line, and Tommy in Matilda JR. Outside of Not Just Dance I've also preformed as a Pirate/Policeman in Rachel Carson's Pirates of Penzance JR.
